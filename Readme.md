# Terminal-Chess

Chess game that runs on a terminal with C# and .Net(**Developed as a study**).

## Instructions

**Obs**: This algorithm has only 3 special chess moves built in: Castling, En Passant and Promotion.

The white pieces are at the bottom and the black ones at the top, to play select the position of the piece you want to move (ex: a2) then the position you want to move it to (ex: a3). Below is a visual example of the chessboard used:

```bash
8 T H B Q K B H T
7 P P P P P P P P
6 - - - - - - - -
5 - - - - - - - -
4 - - - - - - - -
3 - - - - - - - -
2 P P P P P P P P
1 T H B Q K B H T
  a b c d e f g h
```
